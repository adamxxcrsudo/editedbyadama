Brief & Strategy

Every project starts with understanding your vision. I dive deep into your objectives, target audience, and creative direction to ensure the final edit aligns perfectly with your goals.

Organize Footage

Raw footage is carefully logged, tagged, and organized. I create a systematic workflow that makes every asset easily accessible throughout the editing process.

Assembly Edit

The first pass brings your story to life. I construct a rough timeline focusing on narrative flow and pacing, establishing the foundation for the final piece.

Refine & Polish

With precision timing, I refine every cut, transition, and moment. This is where the edit transforms from good to exceptional.

Sound Design

Audio is half the experience. I craft immersive soundscapes, mix dialogue, add effects, and ensure every sonic element enhances the visual story.

Color Grade

Color sets the mood. I apply professional color grading to create the perfect atmosphere, ensuring visual consistency and emotional impact.

Final Delivery

Your project is exported in optimal formats for every platform. From broadcast masters to social media cuts, every deliverable is crafted for maximum impact.
